Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2025

This policy integrates the privacy and cookie terms in compliance with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD Law No. 13,709/2018) and international data protection regulations.

We at MyBud take your privacy seriously. This document explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when using our platform.

1. Legal Bases for Processing

All data processing is based on the following legal bases:

  • Explicit consent (Art. 7, I LGPD)
  • Contract performance (Art. 7, V LGPD)
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 7, IX LGPD)
  • Compliance with legal obligation (Art. 7, II LGPD)

2. Data Collected

  • Personal data: name, email.
  • Technical data: IP, device type
  • Navigation data: visited pages, interactions, session time
  • Metadata: records of actions performed on the platform

3. Cookie Policy

We only use essential and analytics cookies, as detailed below:

Essential Cookies

  • Purpose: authentication, security and basic functions
  • Examples: session maintenance, fraud prevention
  • Control: always active, do not require consent

Analytics Cookies

  • Tool: Google Analytics (anonymized data)
  • We collect: aggregated browsing behavior
  • Duration: 14 months
  • Control: active only with consent

Management: control your cookies through the cookie icon button in the lower right corner of the screen.

4. Purposes of Processing

  • Provide and improve the contracted services
  • Personalize the user experience
  • Facilitate communication with associations, partners and other growers
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Statistical analysis of platform usage
  • Compliance with regulatory obligations

5. Data Sharing

  • Technology partners: hosting and infrastructure
  • Analytics platforms: Google Analytics (anonymized data)
  • Authorities: upon valid court order
  • Service providers: companies that assist in operating the platform under confidentiality agreements
  • Corporate group: for internal administrative purposes

6. Your Rights

  • Full access to stored data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Data portability to other services
  • Deletion of non-essential data
  • Revocation of granted consents

7. Security Measures

  • Encryption for sensitive data
  • Continuous vulnerability monitoring
  • Periodic penetration testing
  • Daily backups

8. Data Retention

  • Registration data: up to 5 years after last interaction
  • Access logs: 6 months
  • Analytics data: 2 years in anonymized format
  • Financial information: 10 years according to tax legislation

9. Policy Updates

We will notify about significant changes through:

  • Email to registered address
  • Public log of changes on our website
